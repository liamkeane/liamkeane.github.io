Carltune

Our goal is to better connect students with Carleton's live music scene (events, bands, socials) through technology. We are exploring the idea of developing an app making live music opportunities on campus accessible, whether that be playing music or finding music to listen to.


My stakeholder described an app capable of filtering by venue, displaying short form media from specific events, and highlighting each bands' unique personality. While this prototype addresses the first two, the third ask is a little more nebulous. My tentative idea is adding about pages or short video introductions.
